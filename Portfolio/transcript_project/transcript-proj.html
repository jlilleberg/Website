Textual Analysis and Language Models of Presidential Speeches of the United States

Political eras in the United States refer to a model of American politics used in history and political science to periodize the political party system existing in the United States. Political scientists and historians have divided the development of America's two-party system into six eras.

For each political era, I have performed data exploration, sentiment analysis, generated texts using neural networks, topic modeling and text data visualizations. The project is separated into the respective categories stated above where each category contains interactive visualizations to allow further insight and exploration into the data.

Important: This project is best viewed on desktop as some plots do not display properly on mobile.

Exploratory Data Exploration
Interactive visualizations on the overview characteristics of presidential transcripts.
Sentiment Analysis
Analysis of emotional and subjective tones of presidential transcripts.
Text Generation using Recurrent Neural Networks
Artificial presidential speech using text generation with recurrent neural networks trained on President Obama's speeches.
Textual Deconstruction & Visualization
In-depth interactive visualizations with topic modeling, term associations, empaths, term and topic frequencies, and word similiarities.
